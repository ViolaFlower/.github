# :wave:  ViolaFlower
Violaflower is an organization of developers seeking to enhance the Minecraft Modding scene with reliable, high quality mods and modpacks.

## :rocket:  Our Ideals
Our ideals consists of preferring Quality over Quantity in terms of our projects. Our most notable projects consist of Re-Console, Deck-Ify and Nitrogen.
We also support FOSS software, seeking to use open source licenses, open source our configs (in the cases of modpacks), whilst making websites or mods open source.

We support this movement as a way to help everyone in the community wanting to fork, build off of, improve, etc. off our affiliated modpacks and mods.
